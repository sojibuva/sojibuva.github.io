Carburized Tool Wear — Root Cause & Heat-Treat Optimization

Caterpillar · 2025–Present

Investigated premature wear in ground-engaging tools using SEM/EDS, hardness mapping, and XRD to detect retained austenite and nonuniform carbon diffusion. Recommended quench and temperature profile adjustments that improved hardness uniformity and wear performance.

  • SEM/EDS
  • XRD
  • Heat Treatment

Dissimilar Welding (A36 ↔ 50CR) — Solidification Cracking Mitigation

University of Virginia · 2023–2025

Mapped process windows to suppress cracking; refined ferrite prediction and filler-metal selection. Employed SEM, EBSD, and CALPHAD to correlate heat input and composition with microstructure and performance.

  • EBSD
  • CALPHAD
  • Weldability

Compressor Block Casting — Process Development & Stabilization

Walton Hi-Tech Industries PLC · 2016–2018

Designed gating/feeding for multiple compressor-block castings on DISAMATIC lines; integrated thermal analysis to control undercooling and stabilize production. Supported export qualification with European and Japanese compressor OEMs.

  • Thermal Analysis
  • Gating/Feeding
  • DISAMATIC

Gas Pipeline Integrity & Field Welding

Titas Gas Transmission & Distribution Co. Ltd. · 2018–2021

Designed gas pipeline sections and supervised field welding, non-destructive testing, and cathodic-protection systems to ensure safe and reliable gas transmission and distribution.

  • Pipeline Design
  • Welding & NDT
  • Cathodic Protection
